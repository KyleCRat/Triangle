A History of the MSU Chapter of Triange


The Michigan State Chapter of Triangle Fraternity was installed on January 8, 1955, as the 20th chapter. This chapter began at MSU with 23 members with John Alquist taking chapter serial number 1. It gained it's first house on 1234 E. Grand River Ave in 1961, eventually moving to it's current home at 242 N. Harrison as it was bought from the Alpha Delta Pi Sorrority that then moved across the street. Since its installation there have been reorganizations of the chapter, but the chapter has never officially disbanded. In the Summer of 2003, the chapter with the help of the College of Engineering, held Leadership Advantage, a summer freshmen orientation program to improve the leadership of the incoming students. With an average of 45 students each summer taking part in the program, the chapter has been able to recruit the best engineering leaders that have come to Michigan State. Today the chapter is above 50 members and is looking to break the 60 member mark within the next few years.

As one of the smaller houses on campus and apart of Triangle we have the greater opportunity to know eachother closely. We are made up with quite a few senior members that have been apart of this chapter for over three years, giving us a large knowledge pool and great strength amongst the Brothers. With growing alumni support and an increasing size of the house, this chapter is rising fast and will continue to rise with the efforts of the great members.
